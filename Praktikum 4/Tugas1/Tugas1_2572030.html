Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A rich and hearty Italian most served over perfectly cooked spaghetti. Simple, comforting, and absolutely delicious.

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 250g ground beef
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian gerbs to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Saute onion and gralic until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in crushed tomatoes and season wit herbs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer on low heat for 20 minutes.
  6. Cook spaghetti accordinf to package instructions
  7. Serve sauce over spaghetti and enjoy!
